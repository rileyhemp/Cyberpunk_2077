About

The real you is not enough

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure story set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour and body modification. You play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. You can customize your character’s cyberware, skillset and playstyle, and explore a vast city where the choices you make shape the story and the world around you.

  • V

    This is the protagonist, which unlike Geralt in The Witcher 3 , we can customize to start the game with a complex character creation system which allows us to choose their sex (in the E3 demo was a woman) to create a argumental background. We can also choose between different classes: Netrunner , Techie , Solo or a mixture of all of them.

  • Dexter
    Deshawn

    Fixers like Dexter juggle illegal contracts—burglaries, assassinations, kidnappings, trafficking, you name it. And Dex is one of the best fixers in town. Hardly anything goes in Night City that he doesn't know about. He’s got killer intuition and the experience to match – word on the street is anyone who manages their first job for Dex is sure to go far.

  • Jackie
    Welles

    As a teenager Jackie always wanted to be his own master, so he eventually settled on a career as mercenary. Friendship, loyalty and family ties are what drives him. To Jackie, blood is always thicker than water. He’s a rock-solid, composed professional who respects other mercs. Eventually he wants to provide for his own family and live in luxury, but he’s getting impatient because this is still just an elusive dream.

  • Johnny
    Silverhand

    Johnny was a war veteran in Central America, but then he changed his mind to Rocker. He became the leader of the popular Samurai group. He is famous for his musical talent and many short, quickly decaying relationships. His partners were Alt Cunningham and Rogue.
